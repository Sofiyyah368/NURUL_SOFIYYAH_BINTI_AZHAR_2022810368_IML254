SPM

Pusat Pendidikan Yayasan Terengganu Kuala Berang (2021)

This school is quite unique as it open for people around 17 to 25 years only to take Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM), so mostly students here are repeated student, a student who not take SPM yet, or people who want to work but need SPM certificate. We here make friends regardless of age, and the vibe is really different from high school.

Additional in my year, Terengganu had facing severe floods and heavy rain that didn't stop, which submerged almost half of our school building right before the SPM exam day.
